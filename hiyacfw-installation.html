    HiyaCFW installation guide written by Robz8

    Make sure to follow the guide closely to ensure that you do everything correctly.

    Make sure to download the right files before proceeding with the guide.

    What you need (PC)

    • Hex editor (HxD is recommended)
    • An .ips patching program
    • WinImage

    What you need (DSi)

    • Latest version of HiyaCFW
    • v1.4 of launcher (named 00000002.app)
    • Your regularly used SD card for DSi
    • A 2GB (or lower) SD card to be used for SDNAND (If your regular SD is 2GB or lower, then that'll work too)
    • Your console ID and CID
    • Unlaunch installed

    Instructions

    1. Extract every file in the .7z file, to a HiyaCFW folder on your PC.
    2. On your PC, put fwTool.nds from the regularly used SD card folder, on your regular SD card.
    3. Rename fwtool.nds to boot.nds.
    4. Put your regular SD card in your DSi.
    5. Turn on your DSi.
    6. Launch your exploited DSiWare.
    7. Dump your NAND using fwTool.
    8. After NAND dumping is done, take the SD card out of your DSi, and put it into your PC.
    9. Go to the FW############ folder.
    10. Copy nand_dsi.bin to the "for PC" folder, where TWLTool is at.
    11. Remove your SD card from your PC.
    12. Decrypt your NAND by running this in cmd: twltool nandcrypt --cid cidhere --consoleid consoleidhere --in "nandpathhere" Replace cidhere with your CID, consoleidhere with your console ID, and nandpathhere with the path of your NAND (ex. C:/NAND folder/nand_dsi.bin).
    13. Get the arm7 and arm9 binaries of bootloader, by running this in cmd: TWLTool boot2 --in "nandpath"
    14. Move the arm7.bin and arm9.bin files to the bootloader files folder, and make backups of them.
    15. Patch both files with their .ips patches.
    16. Name them arm7 (patched).bin and arm9 (patched).bin.
    17. In HxD, open both arm9 (patched).bin and bootloader arm9 append to start.bin files.
    18. In bootloader arm9 append to start.bin, copy the bytes, and paste-insert (not paste-write) it into the start of arm9 (patched).bin.
    19. Save arm9 (patched).bin, and exit HxD.
    20. Run the make bootloader nds.bat file to get your bootloader.nds file!
    21. Grab your SDNAND SD card, and put it into your PC.
    22. Place bootloader.nds on the SD card root.
    23. Open nand_dsi.bin in WinImage, then click OK.
    24. Select Image -> Extract.
    25. Set the SD card root as the path.
    26. Set Extract folders method to Extract with pathname.
    27. Click OK to extract the NAND contents to your SDNAND! When that's done, exit WinImage.
    28. Copy everything in the for 2GB (or lower) SD card (SDNAND) folder, to the SD root. If it asks you to overwrite any files, click Yes.
    29. Decrypt launcher by running this in cmd: TWLTool modcrypt --in "path of 00000002.app" --out "path of 00000002_dec.app"
    30. Patch 00000002_dec.app with v1.4 Launcher (00000002.app) patch.ips.
    31. Copy the patched file to \title\00030017\484e41xx\content on your SDNAND SD card, and rename it to 00000002.app. (xx varies, depending on region) If there's a .app in that folder, with a higher number than 2, delete that .app file. You'll only need "00000002.app", and the .tmd file in that folder.
    32. You're all done! Put your SDNAND SD card into your DSi, turn on your DSi, and when you see the HiyaCFW splash for the first time, that means you're now running HiyaCFW!

    You're now safe from console bricks, and you are now able to run custom DSiWare from the SD card, by installing it's .tia to the SDNAND! You can now also run blocked flashcards!
